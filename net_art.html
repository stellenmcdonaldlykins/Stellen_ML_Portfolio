Net Art

03/14/2026

This project is an interactive net art game inspired by my fascination with scarab beetles. Players guide the development of a beetle larva through a series of environmental choices, ultimately revealing the adult species it becomes. The project invites viewers to reconsider insects and recognize the beauty in creatures that are often overlooked or misunderstood.
