Hello! My name is Stellen McDonald-Lykins. This site is my Art 74 portfolio at San José State University. In this class, we focus on the basics of digital media production, including using visualization software and building work for the web. We also reference Mark Tribe’s idea of new media art as work that uses emerging technologies to explore cultural, political, and aesthetic possibilities. This portfolio shows my experiments with those ideas throughout the course.
